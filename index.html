About

Hello! I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Kim Jaechul Graduate School of AI at KAIST, where I am fortunate to be advised by Jaegul Choo. I am serving as a Technical Research Personnel in the Republic of Korea Army and was previously a Research Intern at Naver Webtoon. My primary research interests lie in the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning, with a focus on information extraction and privacy. Prior to joining KAIST, I received my B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
